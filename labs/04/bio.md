# Biography

Avery Calder is a self-taught technologist and worldbuilder known for blending data science with imaginative storytelling. After growing up fascinated by both fantasy games and machine learning, Avery began creating digital tools that generate unique in-game artifacts and lore. Their projects—ranging from procedural charm systems to AI-driven ecosystems—reflect a passion for turning algorithms into art. When not coding, Avery can be found sketching creature designs or hiking for inspiration in the wilderness.
